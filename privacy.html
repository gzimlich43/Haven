Privacy Policy

With Haven Ai respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. We collect minimal user data to enhance your experience, including app usage metrics and journaling interactions, which may be anonymized and used for internal improvement purposes. We do not sell or share your data with third-party advertisers.

All conversations are stored securely and encrypted where possible. However, no data system is 100% secure, so users should avoid sharing personally identifiable information, medical history, or sensitive health data within the app.
