What is Haven?

Haven is an emotionally intelligent AI support system created specifically for caregivers. It provides compassionate guidance, emotional relief, and practical support—any time of day, without judgment or overwhelm.

Who is Haven for?

Right now, Haven is designed to support caregivers—whether you’re caring for a family member, a partner, or a patient. If you’ve ever felt the weight of holding things together for someone else, Haven is for you.

Can I gift Haven to someone else?

Yes! You can send 1-month, 3-month, or annual access to another caregiver. Visit withhaven.org/gift to learn more.

Is Haven medically accurate?

Haven is informed by current clinical knowledge and caregiving best practices. While not a replacement for a doctor or therapist, it provides grounded, emotionally aware support you can trust—especially during late-night overwhelm.

What makes Haven different from other apps or AI tools?

Haven is purpose-built for emotional caregiving support. It adapts to your tone, remembers what matters, and offers support modes based on how you're feeling. No fluff. No toxic positivity—just honest, intelligent care.

Is my information private?

Absolutely. Your privacy is our priority. Haven does not sell your data, and your conversations are encrypted and secure.
