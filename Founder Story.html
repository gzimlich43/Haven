A Note From Our Founder

"This is more than just an app. Haven was born from one late-night moment where I needed something that didn’t exist. I hope it meets you in that same place."

Dear Caregiver,

I’ve been where you are: exhausted, scared, and carrying more than anyone should have to. I built Haven because I needed someone to talk to when no one else was awake. It started with scribbles in a notebook, turned into midnight coding sessions, and eventually became what you're looking at now. I built this for you, because I wish it existed when I needed it most.

With care,
Heather Ruby
